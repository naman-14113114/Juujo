# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

People shopping for premium bedding who want better sleep and everyday comfort: warm sleepers looking for cooling sheets, restless sleepers considering a weighted blanket, wellness-minded buyers curious about grounding sheets, and anyone upgrading pillows. They shop across the US, UK, Canada, and Australia, compare on quality and trust before buying, and expect clear sizing, honest materials, transparent pricing, and a calm, confidence-building experience.

## Product Purpose

Juujo is a luxury bedding brand focused on comfort and better sleep. The storefront sells a growing range of bedding: Grounding Sheets, Weighted Blankets, Cooling Bed Sheets, and Pillows, with more categories to come. Success means a shopper can quickly understand a product, choose the right colour and size, see the value of buying more than one through a simple quantity offer, and reach checkout with trust intact. The store must feel like it was built as a premium bedding brand from day one, with no trace of any prior product line.

## Brand Personality

Calm, premium, and trustworthy. Juujo feels like a quiet luxury home brand: warm, tactile, and reassuring rather than clinical or loud. The tone is confident and plain-spoken, never hypey. It respects the buyer's intelligence, leads with material honesty and comfort, and treats better sleep as the outcome that matters.

## Anti-references

Avoid the "bedding equals plain beige linen on a white background" cliche, sterile healthcare white-and-teal, aggressive discount-store energy, fake urgency, invented certifications or medical sleep claims, mismatched or obviously stock imagery, identical card grids, gradient text, decorative glassmorphism, and any generic AI landing-page pattern. Never imply clinical outcomes for grounding or weighted products beyond comfort and rest.

## Strategic Principles

- Lead with comfort and better sleep, supported by material honesty (fabric, weave, fill, weight, care).
- Make colour and size selection effortless and make pricing update instantly and clearly.
- Replace gift-bundle mechanics with a clean quantity offer: Buy 1, Buy 2 (save), Buy 3 (best value, recommended).
- Build every product surface to scale to new categories without redesign.
- Earn trust with plain guarantees (night trial, easy returns, free shipping) rather than loud badges.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Readable responsive typography, meaningful alt text on product and lifestyle imagery, keyboard-accessible colour and size selectors with clear focus states, accurate labels for the quantity offer and price changes, sufficient contrast on warm neutrals, and no reliance on colour alone to signal the selected variant or the recommended offer.
